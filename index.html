Walk Me Through It

Walk Me Through It is a navigation system that uses haptic feedback for the visually impaired. Paired with a voice-controlled Android app, the headset uses the phone's GPS and directions from Google's Directions API to make one of two motors vibrate to indicate a left or right turn.
